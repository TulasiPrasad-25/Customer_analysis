📊 Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis
🧠 Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using transactional data to uncover insights into purchasing patterns, customer segments, and revenue drivers. The goal is to support data-driven business decisions in retail.

🎯 Business Problem

A retail company wants to understand how factors like demographics, discounts, and product preferences influence customer purchases and loyalty.

(Reference: Business Problem Document)

⚙️ Tech Stack
Python (Pandas) – Data Cleaning & EDA
SQL (PostgreSQL) – Data Analysis
Power BI – Dashboard & Visualization
🔍 Key Analysis
Revenue by gender and age group
Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
Impact of discounts and subscriptions
Top products and categories
Shipping type comparison

(Detailed in analysis report)

📊 Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard highlights:

Customer distribution
Revenue trends
Category performance
Subscription insights
💡 Key Insights
Loyal customers form the majority
Discounts significantly influence purchases
Young adults contribute highest revenue
Subscription impact on spending is minimal
